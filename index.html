Smart b-roll

B-roll cut in automatically

While you're talking, Slice reads your b-roll and places cutaways where they fit — matching the subject of what you're saying to the shot on screen. The result looks like you spent an hour on it.

A-roll detection

Your best takes, found automatically

Slice transcribes everything you said and finds your strongest talking-to-camera moments — clear speech, good energy, no false starts. Your a-roll spine is built before you touch anything.

Customization

Pick your length. Done.

1 min, 3 min, 5 min, or 10 min — pick your target length and Slice builds to it. Re-edit instantly from the results screen without re-analyzing. Your defaults save automatically.

Premiere XML export

Every vlog exports a Premiere-compatible XML timeline — a-roll on V1, b-roll on V2, all source clips referenced. Open it, refine it, done. SRT sidecar exports automatically alongside.
